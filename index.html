About

A practitioner-led perspective on how organizations adopt, measure, and scale technology.

Vinay Kumar Polamarasetty works across enterprise software, artificial intelligence, and decision systems. His work has contributed to large-scale initiatives spanning ERP, CRM, CPQ, analytics, and AI-enabled solutions across heavy-equipment dealerships in construction, agriculture, and mining. His focus is on translating complex business needs into systems, processes, and technology solutions that improve operational effectiveness at scale. Alongside his industry work, he researches how organizations can evaluate AI adoption, decision quality, and enterprise value. His research has been published through SSRN and cited in IEEE literature.
